About me

I received my B.Eng degree from Lanzhou University of Technology, Lanzhou, China, in 2015, and M.Eng degree from Chongqing University, Chongqing, China, in 2018. I am currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with the School of Computer Science and Technology at Xi'an Jiaotong University, Xi'an, China. I have been visiting the HPC-AI Lab of the National University of Singapore since Apr. 2021 to Apr. 2022.

Research interests

My current research interests are multimodality learning including textbook question answering and visual question answering. My previous research interests span from coreference resolution to imbalanced data classification.
